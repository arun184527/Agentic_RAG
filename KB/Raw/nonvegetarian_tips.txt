Title: Nutrition Guidelines for Non-Vegetarian Weight-Loss Diets

1. Choose lean protein sources to maintain satiety while controlling calorie intake:
   - Skinless chicken, turkey, and lean red meat
   - Fish such as salmon, tuna, sardines, tilapia
   - Eggs and low-fat dairy

2. Benefits of lean protein:
   - Helps maintain muscle mass
   - Supports metabolism
   - Reduces hunger and prevents overeating

3. Healthy cooking methods include:
   - Grilling, baking, steaming, air frying
   - Avoid deep-frying or using heavy oils

4. Limit or avoid processed meats such as:
   - Sausages, salami, bacon, ham
   These are high in calories, sodium, and unhealthy fats.

5. Example balanced non-vegetarian meal:
   - Protein (grilled chicken or fish)
   - Vegetables (broccoli, spinach, bell peppers)
   - Whole grain (brown rice, quinoa, whole-wheat roti)

6. Tips for calorie control:
   - Reduce visible fat on meats
   - Avoid creamy sauces
   - Prioritize vegetables to increase fullness

7. Meal plans for non-vegetarian users should follow lean protein guidelines and avoid unhealthy cooking styles.
